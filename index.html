The Church Of God Ithithanam

"On this rock I will build my church, and the towers of Hell will conquer it. It will not be" (Matthew 16:18).

The Church of God ithithanam is the largest indigenous Pentecostal denomination in Kottayam. It has been 50 years since the ithithanam church started functioning in March 2023. The work of this church, which started in 1973 as a small group, by the grace of God has led many individuals and families to a life of faith.

Church
Service

These are our church services, offering a variety of worship experiences to nurture spiritual growth and community connection. We gather regularly for worship, prayer, and teaching, with Sunday services, including a dedicated Sunday School session every Sunday morning from 9:00 to 10:00. Each service is designed to inspire faith, encourage fellowship, and support the spiritual journey of every participant.


Praise and Worship

every Sunday at 10:00 AM, where we come together as a community to celebrate faith, share in meaningful messages, and uplift one another through prayer and song. All are welcome!


Youth Meeting

Join us for our Youth Meeting every first Sunday of the month at 12 PM! It's a great time for young people to come together, connect, and grow in faith through discussions, activities, and fellowship.


Fasting prayer

A fasting prayer service is held at the end of every month, lasting for three days. During this time, participants dedicate themselves to prayer, fasting, and spiritual reflection. These services aim to deepen faith, seek divine guidance, and promote community bonding.


Sunday School

Sunday School is conducted every Sunday morning from 9:00 to 10:00. It offers a dedicated time for teaching and learning about religious principles, often tailored for children and young adults.
